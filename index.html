  Andrew Corp. is more than 160 years old. The history of our company can be traced back to Andrew Andrewson's 1847 opening of a single shop on London's Bond Street, selling cats and ready-made kittens. In 1902, Andrew Corp. & Co., Ltd. incorporated as a small kitten company in New York City. In 1960, Andrew Corp was the smallest among the six major kitten companies in the United States. By 1983, AC USA had become the largest kitten company in the country.
